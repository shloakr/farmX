Environmental Issues

India ranks 5th in the world's most polluted nations; this leads to immediate repercussion on crop yield and on the health of farmers. India produces a record 100 million tons of wheat harvest in 2018-2019. If the atmosphere was as clean as it was 50 years ago the harvest could have been up to 140 million tones.
